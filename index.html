Welcome

SensingAI, we are a dedicated team passionate about transforming the lives of individuals with visual impairments through the power of assistive technology. Our mission is to create innovative solutions that enhance independence, accessibility, and overall quality of life for the blind community.

We understand the unique challenges faced by individuals with visual impairments in their daily lives. Navigating the world, accessing information, and performing tasks that many take for granted can often be difficult or inaccessible without the right support. That's why we are committed to developing cutting-edge technologies that empower the blind to overcome these barriers and live more fulfilling lives.


Our academic research interests include:

  • Computer Vision Theory
  • Road Segmentation
  • Object Detection
  • Domain Adaption

Our application interests include:

  • Assistive Technology for the Blind
  • Automatic Navigation
  • Off-Road Navigation
  • Motion Planning
